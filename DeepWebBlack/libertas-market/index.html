Libertas Market

Updated December 12th, 2019

Libertas Market is placed in our “Scam Markets” section because the market was involved in an exit scam a year ago. It was the first monero only market. From march to april 2018 market experienced severe outages due to which users werent able to access their funds. Then in late april 2018 the market silently pulled off the exit scam and went away with all the users funds. Now its back after a year thinking people will forget what they did last year.
